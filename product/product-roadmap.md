# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Training Builder
The admin-facing editor for creating and managing training programs — a three-panel interface for authoring slides (video, text, inline questions), configuring quizzes, setting rules pages, and defining recurrence schedules.

### 2. Training Player
The employee-facing experience for completing assigned trainings — mobile-first flow that walks users through slides, enforces rules and timing requirements, and delivers the final quiz.

### 3. Question Bank & Import
A centralized system for managing quiz questions — supports manual creation, bulk CSV import, reusable question pools, and organizing questions by topic or training.

### 4. Analytics & Tracking
Dashboards and reporting for training performance — attempt-level tracking of timing, behavior, and scores with views scoped to individual trainings, employees, and aggregate trends.
