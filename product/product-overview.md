# PeopleOS

## Description
PeopleOS is an end-to-end internal HR operating system for managing the full employee lifecycle. It replaces fragmented HR tooling with one clean, modern, and operationally dense platform that People and Culture teams actually enjoy using — covering training, hiring, scheduling, shift management, time tracking, employee profiles, leave management, and AI-powered automation.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Fragmented HR Tooling
Most teams juggle five or more disconnected tools for training, scheduling, leave, and employee management. PeopleOS consolidates the entire employee lifecycle into a single platform with shared data and unified workflows.

### Problem 2: Bloated Enterprise Software
Legacy HR platforms are slow, cluttered, and painful to navigate. PeopleOS is built for speed and information density — flat, modern UI with strong visual hierarchy and zero dead space.

### Problem 3: No Visibility Into Training Performance
Admins lack granular insight into how employees engage with training content. PeopleOS tracks timing, behavior, and performance at the attempt level, surfacing analytics across training, user, and attempt dimensions.

### Problem 4: Manual and Repetitive HR Operations
Creating trainings, assigning schedules, and tracking compliance involves too much manual work. PeopleOS introduces AI-powered automation and bulk operations (like CSV question import) to eliminate repetitive tasks.

### Problem 5: Poor Employee Experience
Employees are forced to use clunky desktop-first tools on the go. PeopleOS provides a mobile-first employee experience for completing trainings, viewing profiles, and managing their work life.

## Key Features
- Structured training builder with slides (video, text, inline questions) and quiz authoring
- Bulk question import via CSV and reusable question banks
- Attempt-level analytics with timing, behavior, and performance tracking
- Recurring training schedules with automatic reassignment
- Rules page and pre-training expectations configuration
- Employee profiles with lifecycle tracking
- Department and role-based organization
- Desktop-first admin experience with left sidebar navigation
- Mobile-first employee experience
- Card and table-based layouts with high information density
